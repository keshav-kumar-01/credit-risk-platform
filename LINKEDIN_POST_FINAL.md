## 📋 LINKEDIN ALGORITHM OPTIMIZATION TIPS

### Pre-Post Checklist:
- [ ] Post between 8-10 AM IST (Tuesday, Wednesday, or Thursday)
- [ ] Put the GitHub link in the FIRST COMMENT, not the post body
- [ ] Use a carousel (PDF upload) with images 1-7 above
- [ ] Tag 3-5 people and 2-3 companies (not more, LinkedIn deprioritizes spam-tagging)
- [ ] Reply to EVERY comment within the first 2 hours
- [ ] Ask 2-3 friends to comment (not just like) within 30 mins of posting

### Algorithm Hacks:
1. **Hook in first line** — "A bank denied my friend's loan..." creates CURIOSITY
2. **Line breaks** — LinkedIn rewards "dwell time" (how long people read)
3. **No external links in post body** — LinkedIn suppresses posts with links. Put GitHub link in comments
4. **Emoji spacing** — Use emojis as section headers, not randomly
5. **End with a question** — Drives comments, which the algorithm loves
6. **Company tags** — Get your post shown to followers of JPMorgan, Microsoft, etc.
7. **Hashtags at bottom** — 10-15 hashtags is optimal for discovery
8. **Carousel > Single Image** — Carousels get 3-5x more reach on LinkedIn
